I am ISTQB FL certified Test Engineer with a Specialist degree in Information Networks and Telecommunications by education. I have 4 plus years of experience in manual and automation testing. I am working as Automation QA Engineer at GeeksForLess Inc. I have been working in different domains like frontend development, manual/automation testing. As a tester, I have good experience in writing test cases, preparing traceability matrix and writing bug reports. I have successfully performed testing like functional testing, smoke testing, regression testing etc. I have enough knowledge of test Automation tools for quick covering regression test suite of web projects.

For the test automation purposes in the current project, I used TypeScript and AzureDevops for CI/CD. For API testing I used GraphqlPlayground and Postman occasionally JMeter. As a test management system, I worked with Jira Zephyr and AzureDevops. In a previous project, I have automated a major portion of our smoke test suite using Selenium Web Driver and by that, we were able to save lots of effort spent on repetitive work.

2 years ago, I was actively engaged in HTML coding. I have experience in Saas, MySQL, PHP, and developing React.js applications. I use Gitlab and bitbucket for version control and presentation of results.

I love nature, mountain tourism, FPV drone racing. Also, I like to play table games and cook interesting dishes. In a workflow, I like to find out new ways of solving problems. In the future want to try test automation in JAVA.
